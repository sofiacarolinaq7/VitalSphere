VitaStep

VitaSteps are specially designed socks that ensure a secure, comfortable fit while you engage in your virtual exploration. Equipped with motion-tracking technology, they help measure your steps as you complete peaceful challenges like walking through nature or navigating tranquil landscapes, enhancing your immersive experience without demanding strenuous activity.
